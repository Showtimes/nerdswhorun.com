About

Nerds Who Run is a running group for nerds. Doesn't matter if you're a new nerd or a seasoned nerdy runner we're all here to get our nerd on and run. After our nerd run we may hit up a local pub for a beverage and continue our nerd conversations.

Anyone is welcome but we're hoping to foster a group of runners from the technology, science, and engineering industries. So, if you're a nerd looking to get out for a little jog and meet fellow nerds without the awkwardness of events this event is for you!
